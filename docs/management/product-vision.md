# Product Vision

## Project Name

E-commerce Data Quality Pipeline

## Vision

Build an end-to-end data pipeline for Brazilian e-commerce data, transforming raw CSV files into reliable analytical datasets for business decision-making.

The project simulates a real data environment by applying data ingestion, PostgreSQL modeling, analytical transformations, data quality checks, automated tests, QA documentation and business intelligence visualization.

## Target Audience

- Data Analytics recruiters
- Business Intelligence recruiters
- QA recruiters
- Technical reviewers
- Managers interested in reliable business indicators

## Business Problem

E-commerce companies need reliable data to understand sales performance, customer behavior and delivery efficiency.

Raw data often contains inconsistencies, missing values, duplicated records or invalid business rules. Without quality checks, dashboards and reports may lead to incorrect decisions.

## Proposed Solution

Create a structured data pipeline with the following layers:

- Raw: original data loaded into PostgreSQL.
- Staging: cleaned and standardized data.
- Marts: business-oriented analytical tables.

The project also includes data quality rules, automated tests and documented QA practices.

## Value Proposition

Deliver reliable, organized and traceable data for business analysis, reducing manual validation and increasing confidence in decision-making.
