# Quality Policy

This project aims to ensure that data used for business analysis is reliable, consistent, traceable and suitable for decision-making.

Quality will be managed through:

- standardized ingestion and transformation processes;
- documented data quality rules;
- automated validations;
- test execution evidence;
- nonconformity records;
- corrective actions;
- continuous improvement;
- technical decision documentation.

## Quality Principles Applied

- Customer focus: deliver reliable data for business users.
- Process approach: structure the pipeline into clear and controlled stages.
- Evidence-based decisions: use tests and validations to support conclusions.
- Continuous improvement: review failures and improve the pipeline over time.

## Quality Objective

Reduce the risk of incorrect business analysis caused by missing, duplicated, inconsistent or invalid data.
